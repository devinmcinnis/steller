Payment Information
  • Changes
  • Prior to day of departure, change fee per direction, per passenger, is $50 CAD plus applicable taxes and any additional fare difference. Changes can be made up to 6 hours prior to departure.
  • Same-day changes are subject to availability and are permitted only for same-day flights at a fee of $100 CAD per direction, per passenger.
  • Same-day standby is not permitted.
  • Cancellations
  • Tickets are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  • Cancellations can be made up to 6 hours before the flight departure time.
  • Customers who do not show up for their flight will forfeit the fare paid.
